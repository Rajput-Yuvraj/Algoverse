Sliding Window Algorithm

Introduction

The Sliding Window technique involves moving a fixed-size window across a sequence to find a desired result (like maximum sum, average, etc.) efficiently.

Time and Space Complexity

  • Best Case: O(n)
  • Average Case: O(n)
  • Worst Case: O(n)
  • Space Complexity: O(1)
